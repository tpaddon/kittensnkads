What We Do

Original eco-friendly beeswax product company. I use the finest organic beeswax. All production is completed by me, the process is low impact and sustainable.

From healthy cared for bees all the way to the product, Kittens ‘n’ Kads is made with pure intention and ingredients. The beeswax is made by nature’s worker bees. No artificial scents or colours are required.

Sustainability

Almost 300 million tons of plastic is being produced every year. A lot of that plastic is necessary to make useful things that last. But all too often, the plastic we use ends up in the wrong place.

Right now, our oceans are filling up with plastic that degrades into smaller and smaller pieces of microplastics. If nothing changes, by 2050 there will be more plastic waste in our oceans than fish.

Our oceans need a new wave of sustainable solutions. And – just as it’s always been – that change is being driven by people.

The packaging is a biodegradable and microplastic-free material made entirely from renewable sources and FSC-certified wood.
